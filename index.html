Welcome

LIFTED WOMEN CIRCLE

The Lifted Women Circle is a nurturing community for women embarking on new beginnings and thriving together. We empower our members through online training sessions covering a wide range of topics, including personal finance management, career development, entrepreneurship, self-confidence, goal setting, and inspired action. Additionally, we offer a money savings challenge to support women in their journey towards wealth creation and preservation. A lifted woman is secure in her faith in God and embraces all that she is created to be. She can start over. And thrive.

Our story

Lifted Women Circle was born from a vision that began on a simple piece of paper on Thursday, August 3rd, 2023. The visionary, Josephine Wabuu, dreamed of creating a safe space where women could come together to learn, grow, and embrace their unique calling, all while managing their financial resources with wisdom and grace. With a heart full of compassion for women's financial well-being, Josephine has a special place in her heart for those who are starting over in life. The Lifted Women Circle is a beacon of hope, offering a safe and empowering space for women to start over and thrive. With a decade of experience in the banking industry, Josephine's passion for customer experience and excellence shines through in every aspect of the Lifted Women Circle.

The Vision

On 28th December 2023, the first meeting of the Lifted Women Circle was held on Zoom Platform with 47 women plugging in for the casting of the vision. Lifted Women Circle Cohort one has 170 women made up of students, Entrepreneurs, Stay At Home Mums, Career Women, and Professionals. These women are also in different seasons of life, but are brought together with the desire to start over. And thrive. We bring financial literacy in a bold, relevant and relatable way. We believe that women with the right financial information and support, can transform their lives and those around them.

The Program

Lifted Women Circle holds life-changing and impactful monthly trainings online on the Zoom platform. Two physical meetings are also part of the program for the women to interact, network, and fellowship. The sessions we have held so far with local and international facilitators are: “B” is for Budgeting, Elevate in Heels Corporate Training, The Month of Goals and Inspired Action, Entrepreneurship, Personal Branding, and Investing. During the week, we have a daily posting schedule where the women have assigned readings, journaling prompts, sharing of inspirational stories, and devotionals. The day the women look forward to is Envelope Saturday where they save money in cash or digitally, and put an envelope emoji in the group for accountability.
